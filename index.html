About Me

I'm Fortunate. I've been doing hair and makeup for 18 years. Started in my living room, now I run FORFO Studio in Harare. What do I do? Braiding, weaving, locks, wigs, makeup anything. I don't overpromise. I just show up and do good work. My clients stay because I'm honest. If something won't suit you, I'll say it. If it will, I'll make sure you leave happy. No rushed appointments. No hidden prices. Just quality work and good conversation. I treat your hair like it's my own. Every service is personalized to fit you. I stay updated with techniques but never lose the human touch. Your time is valuable, so I respect it. My studio is a judgment-free space. Whether it's a big day or a fresh start, I've got you. Want to see what I can do? Let's connect. Book a session and let's create something beautiful together.
